About

Finding an internship is never easy, especially due to the recent COVID19 pandemic, many opportunities are taken away from students. Internpod is a non-profit site that strive to gather internships and other academic opportunities for college students and lessen the stress and anxiety during this difficult time.

Resources

We have gathered a spreadsheet of 200+ internship opportunities for college freshmen to senior, we post these oppotunites on a daily basis and we will continue to add to our spreadsheet.
